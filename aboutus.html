About Us

Hibachi Buffet offers delicious dining and takeout to Greenville, IL.

Hibachi Buffet is a cornerstone in the Greenville community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
